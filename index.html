About ISRD

ISRD is an applied systems R&D practice that helps solve complex problems in health, learning, technology, and human performance. It publishes articles, blogs, ebooks, and courses that share its methods — and develops practical tools and products that put applied systems science directly into use.

The work spans medical problem-solving (using complexity and attractor concepts for chronic illness), software development, high-stakes training design, expert practice development, business building, and writing. The common thread is applied systems method: read the structure, map the attractors, intervene experimentally, evolve the model.

Scientists don't stop — they focus. This is that focus.
